How to Use the Keyword Grouping Tool

The Keyword Grouping Tool helps you organize and categorize keywords extracted from URLs. With this tool, you can group common and unique keywords for better SEO strategy and content management. Follow the steps below to use it:

1. Paste or Extract Keywords

Start by pasting the list of keywords you have extracted from URLs or by entering keywords directly into the provided text area. You can paste keywords from your clipboard or use the Keyword Extractor tool to gather them automatically.

2. Start Keyword Grouping

Once the keywords are in the text area, click on the "Start Keyword Grouping" button. The tool will process your list of keywords, grouping similar ones together based on relevance and frequency.

3. View the Grouped Keywords

After the tool finishes processing, you will see the output with keywords grouped into common and unique categories. Common keywords will be grouped together, and unique ones will stand alone, making it easy to identify which terms are highly relevant to your content.

4. Copy the Grouped Keywords

If you want to use the grouped keywords, simply click on the "Copy Output" button. This will copy both the unique and common keywords to your clipboard for easy pasting into your content or keyword list.

5. Manage Your Keywords

Once you have copied the grouped keywords, you can manage them however you wish. Organize them into your content strategy, adjust them based on your SEO needs, or add them to your list of target keywords for optimization.

6. Enjoy Organized Keyword Data

The Keyword Grouping tool saves you time by automating the process of keyword categorization. With grouped keywords at your fingertips, you can now focus on improving your content and boosting your SEO strategy.
