Frequently Asked Questions (Slug Generator)

What is the Slug Generator tool?

The Slug Generator tool helps you create SEO-friendly slugs for your URLs. It converts titles or text into clean, readable, and optimized slugs, which are essential for better search engine ranking and user experience.

How do I generate a slug?

To generate a slug, simply enter the title or text in the input field, choose the separator (hyphen or underscore), and click the "Generate Slug" button. The tool will automatically create a clean and SEO-optimized slug for you to use in your URLs.

Can I remove numbers or dots from the generated slug?

Yes! The Slug Generator tool gives you the option to remove numbers, dots, or any unwanted characters from the generated slug. You can choose to exclude these characters for a cleaner and more focused slug.
